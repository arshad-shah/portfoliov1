DEVELOPMENTS IN THE FIELD OF A I


The developement in AI has been relativily slow over the past 2 to 4 decades.
but with the invention of faster and better processing power it is growing at a much faster rate since the turn of the century.

In the past few decades we have came a long way despite the slow developement in the area.
There have been key step development in the field of the AI.
but there is currently no AI that can successfully replicate the full depth and breadth of human skills and cognition.

But humans have made some interesting advances in the field of A I.
Here is a summary of the Advancements humans have made in AI.
Date Description
1952–1956 The birth of artificial intelligence
1956–1974 The golden years
1974–1980 The first AI winter
1980–1987 Boom in the reaserch of AI
1987–1993 Bust: the second AI winter
1993–2011 Narrow AI
2011–present Artificial General Intelligence
